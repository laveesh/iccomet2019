Paper Submission

Authors are requested to submit their manuscript in MS Word in IEEE format at https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=iccomet2019. The abstract of the submitted manuscript should not be more than 200 words. The full length paper should contain: 4-6 Key -words, Figures, Diagrams, Tables, Graphs and References in prescribed format. The maximum number of pages is 4-6. The peer reviewed and selected papers will be considered for publication in UGC approved journal: IJSER [ISSN: 2229-5518] and IJERT [ISSN: 2278- 0181].

Attractions: Presented papers will be considered for the BEST PAPER AWARD
